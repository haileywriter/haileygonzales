Book 1: House of Embers

For any other street rat, the most powerful dragon in the realm claiming you as her rider would be a good thing.

For me, it’s a prison sentence.

It all started with a heist offering enough money for me to give my sister the best medicine she could ask for and our ticket out of the slums.

Of course I wasn’t going to turn it down.

But the joke was on me. Not only was my entire crew turned to ash before my eyes, but I was brought in by Kieran Caddel, the king’s ruthless executioner and captain of the legendary dragon riders.

My execution was supposed to be a symbol for what happens to traitors. And it would have been if a dragon hadn’t stopped it.

So instead of death, the king offered me a spot amongst the dragon riders. The catch? He’s taken my sister hostage and hidden her from me.

I’m surrounded by enemies—inside and outside the kingdom. I can’t let my guard down.

I’ll save my sister no matter the cost.

A Kingdom of Fire and Fury is a steamy, dragon rider romantasy that includes morally gray characters, enemies-to-lovers, political intrigue, an MMC with shadow powers, and touch her and unalive.
