Book 2: Court of Cruel Fae

My fated mate betrayed me. Now I’m his prisoner.

Just when I was about to escape and return home, my fated mate, Prince Eli of the Night Court, intercepted me. And he wasn’t alone. He brought the man who betrayed and manipulated my mom—my father.

After being locked in the dungeon, the Night Court king gave me two options. Become his weapon or die. It wasn’t much of a choice. Even worse, El is my sponsor. If I step out of line, we’ll both be killed. I’m angry and hurt, but I don’t want him dead.

I’m tired of being a victim. It’s time to fight back.

Captive of the Fae is the second book in the Court of Cruel Fae trilogy. It features (slight) enemies to lovers, a broody male, a heroine who comes into her own powers, fated mates, spice, and royal fae!
