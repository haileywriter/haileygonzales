Book 2: Gray Stone Witches

He tried to kill my coven, but I have no idea who he is.

Witches with weak magic are the scorn of mage society. When someone targets us, no one cares.

If we want to find the man terrorizing our coven, we have to do it ourselves. Which means breaking into the cutthroat magic university that rejected me and erased my memories.

The university thinks witches are cowardly and weak. I’ll prove them wrong. I’ll stop the mage targeting us—even if it kills me.

If you like thrilling urban fantasy with slow-burn romance, angst, and found family, then you’ll love Ancient Magic.
