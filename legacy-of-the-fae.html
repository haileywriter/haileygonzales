Legacy of the Fae (Court of Cruel Fae Book Three)

If accepting my destiny means tearing down the fae realm, then so be it.

I don’t know if I trust Ophiuchus, the secret third Court I ran away with.

Their leader wants to work with me, but he’s kept secrets from Eli, my fated mate. Secrets that have shaken Eli to his core. Secrets I’m not sure we can move past from.

But we don’t have a choice. Not while Eli is trapped by a vow he was forced to make—a vow that says he must kill me.

For the first time since I entered the fae realm, I’m in control of my destiny, and I’ll do whatever it takes to save my fated mate and ensure our freedom.

Legacy of the Fae is the final book in the Court of Cruel Fae trilogy.
